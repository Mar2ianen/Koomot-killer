## Tile usage

The public OpenStreetMap tile servers are not intended to be used as a product backend, bulk download source or offline tile cache. Production and distributable builds should use a legal tile source, for example:

- self-hosted vector tiles;
- PMTiles;
- Martin;
- OpenMapTiles;
- Protomaps;
- a commercial tile provider with suitable terms.
